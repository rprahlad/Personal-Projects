Alpha Ursae Minoris (The North Star)

Star(s): a UMi Aa (main star), UMi B, a UMi Ab, a UMi C, and a UMi D

Number of planets: None discovered

Distance from the Earth: 325-425 light years

Age: 7 × 107 years

System Mass: ~7.15 solar masses

Magnitude (Absolute): -3.6(Aa), 3.6(B), 3.1(Ab)
